Carnegie Mellon University's Formula SAE Team

About

Carnegie Mellon Racing (CMR) is the Carnegie Mellon University chapter of the Society of Automotive Engineers. Every year, we build a fully electric race car to compete at Formula SAE International. For two seasons, I served as the team's Vehicle Dynamics Lead, offering support in suspension design, track testing, and data analysis.

Vehicle Dynamics - Design

The 2024 season was our first attempt at a full carbon fiber unibody, and so designing the control arms required finding a balance between vehicle dynamics and physical constraints. Optimum Kinematics was used to evaluate and compare the suspension to previous successful cars.

Vehicle Test & Procedure

The 2023 season saw a complete overhaul of our vehicle testing procedure with the creation of a JIRA-powered ledger system. System leads were able to easily submit tests to be reviewed by our race operations team and then were completed by priority. I enjoyed leading the team that developed and implemented this new, efficient testing system.
